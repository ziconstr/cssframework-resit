Authentic Mexican Tacos

By Chef José • Apr 22, 2025

Fresh, flavorful tacos with juicy seasoned meat, crisp toppings, and soft tortillas. A street-food favorite made simple!

Ingredients

  • 8 Small tortillas
  • 300g Ground beef or chicken
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Tomato, diced
  • Lettuce, shredded
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Salsa and lime wedges for serving
