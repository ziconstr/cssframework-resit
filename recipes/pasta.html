Delicious Pasta

By Chef John • Apr 17, 2025

This classic pasta dish is full of rich tomato flavor, with a touch of garlic and herbs. It's a comforting and satisfying meal you can whip up in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)
