Indonesian Fried Rice

By Chef Rini • Apr 21, 2025

A spicy and savory fried rice dish with a bold kick from sambal and kecap manis. A beloved staple in Indonesian households.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tsp sambal oelek
  • Fried egg (optional topping)
